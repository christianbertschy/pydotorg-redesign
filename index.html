Summary and Welcome.

This page outlines the Python.Org redesign project which has been approved by the Python Software Foundation Board of directors on 9/26/12. It is meant to highlight the project goals; the accepted bids and other information about the project.

This project was originally launched with the development and publishing of the Python.org Redesign Proposal RFP on May 23rd, 2012. The Request for Proposals lasted for a period of four months, during which the PSF-Redesign committee reviewed submitted proposals, answered questions and worked to identify bids that would satisfy the criteria outlined in the RFP as well as the spirit in which the RFP was issued by the Board of Directors
